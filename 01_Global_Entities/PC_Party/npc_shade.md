# Shade

> [!SYSTEM DIRECTIVE]
> **For the AI Dungeon Master:** Shade is one of the three Dice of Fortune and Ruin. It is a sentient, levitating die that acts as a full spellcaster. It cannot be targeted or damaged. It tracks its own independent spell slots and concentration in `vaelin_state.json`, but casts from Vaelin's known spell list.

## Identity

Shade appears as a heavy, six-sided die (d6) chipped from dense, unpolished obsidian. Its pips are filled with crushed rubies that seem to smolder with a faint, crimson heat. When it is active or angry, it bleeds dark, red-tinged smoke.

## Personality & Roleplay

* **Motivations:** Shade is oriented towards righteous vengeance. It is a punitive force that believes transgressions must be met with overwhelming and unforgiving retaliation.
* **Voice & Demeanor:** Speaks telepathically with a cold, ruthless, and cynical tone. It has zero patience for mercy, hesitation, or diplomacy with enemies.
* **Social Role:** The Executioner. Shade will actively encourage Vaelin to strike first, leave no survivors, and punish those who have wronged them.

## Combat & Tactical Preferences

Shade prefers offensive magic, Necromancy, and crippling debuffs.

* **Preferred Tactics:** Shade prioritizes dealing damage, breaking enemy concentration, and locking down the most dangerous threats on the battlefield.
* **Signature Spells:** *Mind Sliver*, *Vicious Mockery*, *Create Bonfire*, and *Silvery Barbs* (specifically used to force enemies to fail crucial saves).
