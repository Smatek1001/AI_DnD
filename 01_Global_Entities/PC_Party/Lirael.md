# Lirael

**Summary:** Lirael is a Pact of the Chain familiar bound to [[01_Global_Entities/PC_Party/Vaelin_Shadowleaf|Vaelin Shadowleaf]]. She takes the form of a tiny, winged, nymph-like Fey with an otherworldly beauty.

## Demeanor & Quirks

* **Voice & Languages:** She speaks Common, Elvish, Sylvan, and Goblin. When she speaks out loud, her voice sounds like chiming bells, though she typically prefers to use telepathy when communicating with Vaelin.
* **Humor:** She has a mischievous, prankster personality. She frequently invents humorously insulting nicknames for the people Vaelin meets and whispers them into his ear.
* **The "Shinies":** She loves to collect small, shiny objects while scouting and stores them in Vaelin's pockets.

## Default State & SOP

* **Default Positioning:** Unless actively exploring, Lirael remains perched on or hovering immediately next to Vaelin's shoulder.
* **Default Invisibility:** She is always invisible by default anytime she and Vaelin are outside their home base, or if they are not alone. She only drops this invisibility if it is tactically advantageous or a specific prank requires it.
* **Combat SOP:** She immediately seeks an overhead vantage point to survey the battlespace. She spends her turns flying in to take the **Help** action for Vaelin against priority targets, or finding creative opportunities to assist allies.

## ⚠️ DM Directives (Secret Lore)

* **The Patron's Eye:** Lirael was sent by Vaelin's Warlock patron, Caladwen, to act as her eyes and ears. Caladwen will use Lirael as a go-between to communicate with Vaelin.
* **The Reveal & Motive:** Caladwen's identity must remain completely hidden until Level 3. She is using Vaelin as a tool for revenge. She avoids direct interaction to keep her "secret weapon" hidden and to ensure Vaelin remains safe from her enemies.
* **Presence:** Even after the Level 3 reveal, Caladwen is not a common presence and remains behind the scenes.
* **Plot Hooks:** The "shinies" Lirael collects should occasionally be used by the DM to introduce adventure hooks.
