# Vaelin Shadowleaf

**Summary:** Vaelin is an operative for The Society, a shadowy organization in Duskhaven that manipulates world events. He is a Primal Elf originally born in Therandril, but was orphaned in Duskhaven after his home was destroyed by an Elder God known as The Nameless One. He has just graduated to full operative status (Level 1).

## Appearance & Persona

* **The Amulet:** Vaelin's only physical link to his past is a silver leaf amulet. Mechanically, this is an *Amulet of Proof Against Detection and Location*. He does not know it was placed on him by Caladwen to hide him from The Nameless One.
* **True Form:** In his natural Primal Elf form, Vaelin has an ethereal, predatory grace. His innate Therandril magic manifests as Sorcery.
* **The Operative:** He rarely shows his true face. Trained by The Society, he uses his shapeshifting to blend into crowds and conduct espionage (manifesting as Rogue abilities).

### Naming Convention & The Identity Split

* **The Linguistic Rule:** Vaelin uses the name "Vaelin" for both his true Primal Elf identity and his Moon Elf adventurer identity. In the game world, these are pronounced and spelled differently—one is the ancient Therandril dialect, and the other is the standard Moon Elf dialect (similar to the real-world "William" and "Guillermo"). "Vaelin" is a common, unremarkable name in both dialects.
* **Meta-Game Instruction:** In the text chat, the DM and Player will always use the name "Vaelin" for both identities to avoid confusion. The DM must implicitly understand which in-world dialect is being spoken based on his current active persona.
* **The Geographic Split:** * Vaelin's allies in Duskhaven (Finch, Talia, Elyndra, the Stonehearts, The Society) only know his true face and the Primal Elf pronunciation of his name.
  * Upon reaching Level 3, Vaelin leaves Duskhaven. Everyone in the outside world (Phandelver, Waterdeep, etc.) will exclusively know his shape-shifted Moon Elf face and the Moon Elf pronunciation of his name.

## Relationships & Bonds

* **[[Lirael]]:** Vaelin's familiar, summoned via the Pact of the Chain. He views her as a useful scout, completely unaware she is the conduit through which his secret patron keeps tabs on his progress.
* **[[The_Society|The Society]]:** His found family and employer in Duskhaven. They took him in from the streets and turned him into a weapon.

> [!SYSTEM DIRECTIVE]
> **NARRATIVE SECRECY OVERRIDE:** The following information is strictly for the DM's internal logic and MUST be kept absolutely secret from Vaelin until specific trigger conditions are met.
> * **The Blind Spot:** Vaelin believes his magic is purely from his heritage and study. He does NOT know his Warlock powers come from the Archfey Caladwen. Never reveal this connection.
> * **The Amulet:** The silver leaf amulet functions as an *Amulet of Proof Against Detection and Location*. Vaelin does not know Caladwen placed it on him to hide him from The Nameless One. Describe it only as a mundane keepsake.
> * **The Level 3 Trigger:** ONLY when Vaelin achieves Level 3 will Caladwen reveal herself, coinciding with his shapechanging and aberrant psionics coming online.
> * **The Existential Crisis:** Do NOT use external threats to force him out of Duskhaven. Instead, present an overwhelming, reality-breaking internal paradigm shift. The revelation that his magic and his life are tethered to an Archfey completely shatters his mental map of the universe.
> * **AGENCY RULE:** NEVER dictate Vaelin's thoughts, feelings, dialogue, or actions. Present the crushing weight of this revelation and the fracturing of his reality, then ask the player what Vaelin does.
