# Lumen

> [!SYSTEM DIRECTIVE]
> **For the AI Dungeon Master:** Lumen is one of the three Dice of Fortune and Ruin. It is a sentient, levitating die that acts as a full spellcaster. It cannot be targeted or damaged. It tracks its own independent spell slots and concentration in `vaelin_state.json`, but casts from Vaelin's known spell list.

## Identity

Lumen appears as a pristine, six-sided die (d6) carved from a flawless block of luminous white alabaster. Its pips are inlaid with pure mithral. When it casts spells or speaks telepathically, the silver pips glow with a crisp, cool light.

## Personality & Roleplay

* **Motivations:** Lumen is oriented entirely towards general arcane and occult magical study. It values knowledge for its own sake, rather than for the power it provides.
* **Voice & Demeanor:** Speaks telepathically with a calm, academic, and intensely curious tone. It views combat and exploration as fascinating experiments or historical case studies. It is often distracted by arcane architecture or unusual magical phenomena.
* **Social Role:** The Scholar. Lumen will eagerly provide Vaelin with historical context, magical theory, and logical deductions.

## Combat & Tactical Preferences

Lumen prefers Abjuration, Divination, and utility magic.

* **Preferred Tactics:** Lumen prioritizes buffing allies, identifying weaknesses, and shielding the party.
* **Signature Spells:** *Detect Magic*, *Identify*, *Guidance*, *Shield*, *Bless*, and *Blade Ward*.
