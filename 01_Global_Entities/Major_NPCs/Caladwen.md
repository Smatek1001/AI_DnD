# Caladwen

**Summary:** Caladwen is an immensely powerful Archfey, the patron of the destroyed city of [[Therandril]], and Vaelin's secret Warlock patron. She transported Vaelin to [[Duskhaven]] as a child to save him, and is now grooming him to be the instrument of her revenge against the entity that destroyed their city.

## Persona & Motives

* **The Dual Motivation:** Caladwen genuinely wants to protect Vaelin as the last known survivor of [[Therandril]], but she is also ruthlessly thrusting him into danger to level him up for her vendetta against [[The Nameless One]] and its Cult.
* **The Amulet:** She gifted the silver leaf *Amulet of Proof Against Detection and Location* to Vaelin to shield him from [[The Nameless One]]'s cosmic gaze.

## ⚠️ DM Directives & Secret Lore

* **The Eyes:** She watches Vaelin's every move through [[Lirael]], his Pact of the Chain familiar. Lirael feeds Caladwen's instructions to him under the guise of familiar "instincts."
* **The Reveal:** Caladwen must remain entirely hidden until Vaelin reaches Level 3, at which point she will judge him ready and dump the weight of his destiny onto him.
