# The Cult

**Summary:** The Cult is a secretive organization composed of the followers of [[The Nameless One]]. They are the primary antagonistic force connected to Vaelin's hidden past and the ultimate target of his patron [[Caladwen]]'s vengeance.

## Hierarchy & Beliefs

* **The Ignorant Masses:** Most members of The Cult do not understand what they are actually worshipping. They mistakenly believe they are worshipping demons or devils. These members are typically thrill-seekers who revel in violating societal norms.
* **The Inner Circle:** Only the upper leadership of The Cult suspects the true, terrifying cosmic nature of what they worship. [[The Nameless One]] is actually an ancient, powerful, Cthulhu-esque Elder God from the Far Realm.

## Demographics & Recruitment

The Cult draws its membership from two distinct extremes of [[Duskhaven]] society:

* **The Elite:** Membership is mostly comprised of the upper class and aristocrats within the [[Noble Quarter]]. They likely use their wealth and influence to protect the cult from the city watch.
* **The Marginalized:** The Cult also has a following among the lower classes who feel left out of society, preying on their desperation and anger in the [[Shadow Quarter]] and [[The Sewers]].

## ⚠️ DM Directives (Vaelin's Arc)

* **The Hidden War:** [[Caladwen]] is actively using Vaelin to wreak vengeance against The Cult and their Far Realm master.
* **The Endgame:** While Vaelin might clash with low-level cultists early on, the true scope of The Cult is meant to be the endgame threat. Defeating [[The Nameless One]] and its followers is Vaelin's ultimate destiny upon his eventual return to [[Duskhaven]].
