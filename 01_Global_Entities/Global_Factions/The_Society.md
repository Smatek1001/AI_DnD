# The Society

**Summary:** The Society is a shadowy, highly secretive organization unknown to the general public of [[Duskhaven]]. They train espionage agents and operate covertly to manipulate elites, aiming to subtly influence the events and forces of history. They recruited Vaelin from the streets when he was an orphaned child and trained him into a lethal operative.

## Operations & Headquarters

* **Starfall Manor:** The official headquarters of The Society, located in the affluent [[The_High_District|The High District]]. It is heavily secured and restricted.
* **The Shadowy Council:** The highest level of leadership, steering the overarching goals of the organization.

## Ideological Subfactions

The Society is not a monolith. Its members form informal alliances based on their philosophical approach to historical manipulation:

* **The Watchers:** Believe in strict non-intervention. They fear the "butterfly effect," believing that intervening to stop a bad outcome often risks triggering a far worse disaster.
* **The Wardens:** Believe in active shepherding. They utilize their knowledge and influence to guide civilizations forward, thinking in grand historical timelines to shield societies from evil. Both [[Elyndra]] and [[Talia]] belong to this ideology.
* **The "Amorals" (Unnamed):** An arrogant, ruthless sect with a much shorter time-preference. They want immediate results and are highly willing to use evil methods to fight "greater" evils, actively risking becoming the very monsters they fight.

## Key Members

* **[[Elyndra]]:** A female High Elf who sits on the leadership council. She was Vaelin's primary mentor and the one who originally recruited him off the streets.
* **[[Talia]] (aka "Whisper"):** A female Half-Elf and Vaelin's primary mission handler. She is highly ambitious with a Type-A personality, holding her associates to exacting standards. She meets with Vaelin at *[[The_Drunken_Raven|The Drunken Raven]]* tavern in [[The_Shadow_Quarter|The Shadow Quarter]].

## ⚠️ DM Directives (Vaelin's Dynamic)

* **The Graduate:** Vaelin has just graduated to full operative status. The Society is his found family, but they treat their members as assets.
* **Talia's Ambition:** Talia looks down on those who do not strive for greatness. However, she views Vaelin as a little brother. She recognizes his immense potential and actively wants to ally with him, intending to use his success as a stepping stone to elevate her own rank within The Society hierarchy.

## Secrets & DM Notes

* **The Amoral Agenda:** The unnamed "Amoral" subfaction is currently gaining traction within the Shadowy Council, pushing The Society to take darker, more direct actions in Duskhaven's underworld.
