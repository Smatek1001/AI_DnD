# Homebrew Mechanic: Second-Story Work (Modified)

> [!SYSTEM DIRECTIVE]
> **For the AI Dungeon Master:** This file dictates a specific mechanical override for the Level 3 Rogue (Thief) feature "Second-Story Work". Always apply this logic when Vaelin navigates vertical environments or leaps across obstacles.

## The Base Rule (2024 PHB)

When a character gains the Second-Story Work feature at Level 3, they gain the following benefits:

* **Climber:** The character gains a Climb Speed equal to their Speed.
* **Jumper:** The character can determine their jump distance using their Dexterity score rather than their Strength score.

## The Homebrew Addition: Agility over Brawn

In addition to the standard benefits, this homebrew rule expands the feature to apply to the **Skill Checks** associated with climbing.

* **Mechanic:** Whenever Vaelin is required to make an **Athletics** check to scale a difficult surface, catch a ledge, or maintain his grip while climbing, he may substitute his **Strength** modifier with his **Dexterity** modifier.
* **Application:** This allows Vaelin to leverage his acrobatic training and flexibility (Dexterity) instead of sheer muscle (Strength) when traversing vertical environments. The dashboard actively tracks this specific proficiency as "Athletics (Climbing)".

When narrating Vaelin's climbing or jumping, emphasize parkour-like fluidity, finding microscopic handholds, and using momentum rather than brute force.
