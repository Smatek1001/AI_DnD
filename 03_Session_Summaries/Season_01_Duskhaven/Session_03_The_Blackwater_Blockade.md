# Session Summary: The Blackwater Blockade

## Mission Overview

* **Operative:** Vaelin Shadowleaf (Level 2 Gestalt Rogue-Sorlock)
* **Client / Ally:** [[Finch]] (The Ratlings)
* **Target:** Gage, Maddox, and the [[Rust_Dogs|Rust Dogs]]
* **Objective:** Dismantle a Rust Dog extortion checkpoint at Blackwater Alley and recover the coin stolen from Finch's lookouts.

## Narrative Beats

1. **The Briefing:** Finch informed Vaelin at [[The_Nest]] that the Rust Dogs had set up a checkpoint at the border of [[The_Docks]] and the [[Shadow_Quarter]] to extort the Ratlings and local foot traffic.
2. **Reconnaissance:** Leveraging the freezing rain and the darkness of the Shadow Quarter, Vaelin scaled the rooftops above Blackwater Alley to observe the checkpoint, identifying Gage, Maddox, and two grunts.
3. **Psychological Warfare:** Rather than initiating combat, Vaelin used *Control Flames* (via Subtle Spell) to plunge the checkpoint into near-total darkness, instantly triggering Gage's severe phobia of fire/magic. Vaelin then cast *Minor Illusion* to flood the pitch-black alley with the sound of a swarming rat plague.
4. **The Fracture:** With the Rust Dogs panicked, Lirael used her Audio Mimicry while invisible to impersonate Maddox and the grunts. She stoked Gage's paranoia until he accused Maddox of mutiny and attacked him. Maddox retaliated by knocking Gage unconscious with his heavy iron maul, causing the terrified grunts to flee.
5. **The "Guards":** As Maddox attempted to gather the spilled extorted coin, Vaelin descended to the street. He cast *Silent Image* and *Disguise Self* to appear as two members of the City Watch, while Lirael mimicked the sound of approaching reinforcements. Outnumbered and caught red-handed, Maddox dropped the coin and fled into The Docks.
6. **Debrief:** Vaelin recovered the ~15 gp and returned it to Finch at The Nest. The flawless, zero-footprint operation deepened The Ratlings' loyalty to Vaelin before he returned to [[The_Black_Oven]] to rest for the night.

## Session Rewards & Progression

* **Loot:** ~15 gp (Recovered and returned to Finch/The Ratlings). Net personal wealth unchanged.
* **XP Awarded:** 400 Gross XP. After the 4x Gestalt Tax, Vaelin netted **100 XP**.
