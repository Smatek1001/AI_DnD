# Session 1 Summary: The Crow's Nest Heist

**Overview** Vaelin successfully graduates from The Society's espionage training program and officially begins his career as a full operative. After summoning his new fey familiar, Lirael, Vaelin meets his handler and executes a flawless, zero-alarm heist against the Harbor Syndicate, securing a highly confidential encoded ledger while framing a rival gang.

**Key Events & Plot Points**

- **The Summoning:** In his cramped attic safehouse, Vaelin performs an arcane ritual to summon his familiar, a Tinker Bell-esque Fey with dragonfly wings named Lirael. Lirael reveals a sassy, arrogant demeanor and immediately proves her worth by pickpocketing a brass button from a Harbor Syndicate enforcer on her way across the veil.
- **The Briefing:** Vaelin travels to _The Drunken Raven_ to meet his handler, Talia. She tasks him with infiltrating a Harbor Syndicate tavern known as _The Crow's Nest_ to steal an encoded shipping ledger belonging to a paranoid spymaster known as "The Shade".
- **The Infiltration:** * Lirael turns into a spider to scout the ground floor, identifying a dozen heavily armed thugs and a guard at the stairs.
    - Vaelin climbs stacked ale kegs in a blind alley to reach the second-floor office window.
    - Working in tandem, Lirael slips through a drafty gap to smudge a silver Abjuration rune on the inside latch, effectively killing the magic ward, while Vaelin picks the physical lock from the outside.
- **The Safe:** * Using _Detect Magic_, Vaelin ignores an illusory decoy ledger on the desk.
    - He locates a trapped wall safe hidden behind a heavy iron filing cabinet resting on greased casters.
    - After discovering a scrap of nautical chart with the numbers **14 Right, 32 Left, 7 Right** hidden in a false-bottom drawer, Vaelin confirms the combination via micro-abrasions on the brass dial.
    - Standing 15 feet back, Vaelin uses _Mage Hand_ to turn the dial, bypassing the lethal Evocation payload and retrieving the true ledger.
- **The Frame-Up & Escape:** Vaelin wedges an iridescent feather belonging to Magpie Miri's Gang into the caster tracks to frame them for the burglary. He safely exits the warded window, drops the ledger into the coal chute at _The Drunken Raven_, and returns to his hideout.

**Rewards & Status Updates**

- **Experience:** Earned **500 Gross XP** (300 for the primary objective, 100 for covert execution, 100 for framing Magpie Miri's Gang).
- **Rest:** Completed a 4-hour elven Trance (Long Rest). All spell slots, HP, and Hit Dice are fully restored.
- **Loot:** Vaelin's inventory remains unchanged, strictly following Talia's orders not to steal syndicate gold.

**Notable Factions & NPCs**

- **Lirael:** Vaelin's newly summoned Fey familiar, providing critical scouting and magical assistance.
- **Talia:** Vaelin's ambitious Society handler.
- **The Shade:** A paranoid Harbor Syndicate spymaster and owner of the stolen ledger.
- **Magpie Miri's Gang:** An opportunistic crew of dock rats successfully framed for the heist.
