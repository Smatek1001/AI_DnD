# Session Summary: The Blacksalt Breach

## Mission Overview
* **Operative:** Vaelin Shadowleaf (Level 2 Gestalt Rogue-Sorlock)
* **Client / Ally:** Magpie Miri, [[Finch]] (The Ratlings)
* **Target:** [[Harbor_Syndicate]] lockup in Blacksalt Alley
* **Objective:** Infiltrate a Harbor Syndicate lockup, rescue Pip the Goblin, and secure leverage over Magpie Miri.

## Narrative Beats
1. **The Briefing:** Vaelin began the morning by meeting with Magpie Miri at the Rusty Anchor. Posing as a desperate street-thug named "Jonsi," he convinced her to hire him for a high-stakes rescue mission. 
2. **Reconnaissance:** Using intel provided by Finch and the Ratlings, Vaelin tracked the captive goblin, Pip, to a windowless storehouse in Blacksalt Alley. Lirael scouted the area in spider and owl forms, observing the Syndicate's top enforcer, Korgal "Iron Fist."
3. **The Ruse:** Vaelin used *Disguise Self* to perfectly mimic Korgal’s imposing Orcish frame. Combined with Lirael’s flawless audio mimicry, "Korgal" stormed the warehouse and ordered the guards to abandon their posts, claiming Miri had been spotted in Market Square. This effectively cleared the building of all five enforcers.
4. **The Heist:** While alone in the warehouse, Vaelin picked the Syndicate’s heavy iron lockbox, securing 45 gp in extorted tariff coin.
5. **Debrief & Exfiltration:** Vaelin successfully extracted a battered Pip and delivered him to Miri at a derelict cooperage. He earned her powerful debt, plus an additional 50 gp and 2 uncut water-opals. Vaelin then returned to The Nest and paid a 20 gp finder's fee to Finch, cementing his status as a reliable patron of the street urchins, before resting at The Black Oven.

## Session Rewards & Progression
* **Loot:** 45 gp from the lockbox, plus 50 gp and 2 Uncut Water-Opals from Miri, minus the 20 gp finder's fee. **Total: 75 gp (Net total) and 2x Uncut Water-Opals.**
* **XP Awarded:** 750 XP. This includes 400 XP for Tactical Infiltration & Disguise, 200 XP for Social Engineering, and 150 XP for Securing the Ratling Alliance.
* **Key Intel / New Alias:** Vaelin established the alias "Jonsi" and learned the Harbor Syndicate is aggressively targeting Miri's gang due to a framed heist involving a magpie feather.
