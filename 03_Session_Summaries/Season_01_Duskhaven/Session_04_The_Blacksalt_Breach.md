# Session 4 Summary: The Blacksalt Breach

**Location:** The Docks (Blacksalt Alley) & The Shadow Quarter (The Nest)

**Objective:** Infiltrate a Harbor Syndicate lockup, rescue Pip the Goblin, and secure leverage over Magpie Miri.

## **The Operation**

Vaelin began the morning by meeting with **Magpie Miri** at the Rusty Anchor. Posing as a desperate street-thug named **"Jonsi,"** he convinced her to hire him for a high-stakes rescue mission. Miri’s gang had been decimated by the **Harbor Syndicate** following a framing incident at the Crow’s Nest.

Using intel provided by **Finch and the Ratlings**, Vaelin tracked the captive goblin, Pip, to a windowless storehouse in Blacksalt Alley.

## **The Infiltration**

After scouting the area with **Lirael** (in spider and owl form) and observing the Syndicate's top enforcer, **Korgal "Iron Fist,"** Vaelin executed a daring psychological hit:

- **The Disguise:** Vaelin used _Disguise Self_ to perfectly mimic Korgal’s imposing Orcish frame.
- **The Ruse:** Combined with Lirael’s flawless audio mimicry, "Korgal" stormed the warehouse and ordered the guards to abandon their posts. He claimed Miri had been spotted in Market Square and commanded an immediate sweep, effectively clearing the building of all five enforcers.
- **The Loot:** While "Korgal" was alone in the warehouse, Vaelin picked the Syndicate’s heavy iron lockbox, securing **45 gp** in extorted tariff coin.

## **The Aftermath**

Vaelin successfully extracted a battered Pip and delivered him to Miri at a derelict cooperage. The operation resulted in:

1. **A Powerful Debt:** Magpie Miri now owes "Jonsi" a significant favor.
2. **Financial Gain:** Miri paid an additional **50 gp** and **2 uncut water-opals** for the rescue.
3. **Community Standing:** Vaelin returned to The Nest and paid a **20 gp finder's fee** to Finch, cementing his status as a reliable and generous patron of the street urchins.
4. **Syndicate Chaos:** The Harbor Syndicate is currently in disarray, with Korgal hunting ghosts in Market Square and a "robbed" vault he cannot explain.

- **Gold Earned:** +75 gp (Net total)
- **Items:** 2x Uncut Water-Opals
- **New Alias:** Jonsi (The "Desperate" Rogue)

**Status:** Vaelin is currently resting at **The Black Oven**, undetected and wealthy.

For successfully executing a flawless heist, avoiding direct combat with a much stronger foe (Korgal), and securing valuable political leverage on the streets, Vaelin earns **750 XP**.

- **Tactical Infiltration & Disguise:** 400 XP
- **Social Engineering (Tricking the Guards & Intimidating Miri):** 200 XP
