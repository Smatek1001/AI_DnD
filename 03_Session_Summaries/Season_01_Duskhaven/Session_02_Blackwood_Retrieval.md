# Session Summary: The Blackwood Retrieval

## Mission Overview

* **Operative:** Vaelin Shadowleaf (Level 1 Gestalt Rogue-Sorlock)
* **Handler:** [[Talia]] (The Society)
* **Target:** [[Silas_Blackwood]], owner of *[[Blackwoods_Baubles|Blackwood's Baubles]]* in Market Square
* **Objective:** Retrieve a compromised silver signet ring containing a microscopic list of [[The_Noble_Quarter|Noble Quarter]] safehouses before it could be sold to the [[Nightshade_Guild|Nightshade Guild]].

## Narrative Beats

1. **The Briefing:** Vaelin met Talia at [[The_Drunken_Raven|The Drunken Raven]] in the Shadow Quarter. She assigned him his first official mission as a full operative of The Society, emphasizing that the City Watch could not be involved.
2. **Reconnaissance:** Posing as a wealthy buyer, Vaelin scoped out the ground floor of Blackwood's Baubles while Lirael (in invisible spider form) hitched a ride on Silas to the second floor. Lirael observed Silas opening a heavily warded Duergar safe, noting the mechanical combination and the harsh Duergar command word used to drop the Abjuration magic.
3. **The Infiltration:** Under the cover of a freezing coastal storm and deep night, Vaelin scaled the adjacent weaver's shop to reach the roof. To avoid triggering an alarm or waking Silas by opening the dormer window, Vaelin used *Fey Step* to teleport directly into Silas's third-floor bedroom.
4. **The Heist:** Leaving Lirael to monitor the sleeping jeweler, Vaelin descended to the dark workspace. He successfully mimicked the Duergar command word to drop the vault's ward, inputted the mechanical sequence, and secured the signet ring alongside 45 untraceable gold pieces.
5. **The Exfiltration:** As the cold draft began to wake Silas, Vaelin dismissed Lirael to her pocket dimension and used his final *Fey Step* to teleport from a second-floor window directly to the alley below, bypassing all mundane traps.
6. **Debrief & Rewards:** Vaelin delivered the ring to Talia at The Drunken Raven, impressing her with a flawless, zero-footprint execution. He earned his 50gp operational fee and retired to The Black Oven to rest.

## Session Rewards & Progression

* **Loot:** 45 gp (Stolen from Silas), 50 gp (Operational Fee from Talia). **Total: 95 gp.**
* **XP Awarded:** 1,200 Gross XP. After the 4x Gestalt Tax, Vaelin netted **300 XP**.
* **Level Up:** Vaelin has reached **Level 2**! (Unlocking Cunning Action, Font of Magic, Action Surge, and expanded spells/invocations).
