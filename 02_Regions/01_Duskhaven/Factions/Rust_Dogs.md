# The Rust Dogs

**Summary:** The Rust Dogs are a petty criminal gang. They operate out of [[The Docks]].

## Operations & Relations

* **Modus Operandi:** Smash-and-grab, muggings, and low-level extortion. They lack the sophistication of the major syndicates.
* **Street Bullies:** They are known to sometimes bully [[The Ratlings]]. This makes them a direct, street-level annoyance to Vaelin's intelligence network.

## Gang Members

* **Gage (Leader):** A large, arrogant male human. He has a distinct phobia of fire.
* **Maddox:** The second in command. He is a male human who is cruel and brutish.
* **Sparky:** A male human tinkerer. He is nervous and cowardly.

## Secrets & DM Notes

* **Gage's Fear:** Gage's phobia of fire stems from a past encounter with a magic user. It can be heavily exploited to intimidate him.
* **Syndicate Pawns:** The Rust Dogs think they are independent, but they are frequently manipulated into doing the dirty, disposable work for the [[Harbor Syndicate]].
