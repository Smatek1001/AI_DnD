# Ebon Web Syndicate

**Summary:** The Ebon Web Syndicate is a powerful criminal organization run entirely by Drow. They operate out of the expansive sewer network beneath [[Duskhaven]].

## Operations & Strategy

* **Modus Operandi:** Surgical, patient, and terrifyingly precise. They do not break legs; they make people disappear.
* **The Middlemen:** They regulate the illicit connections between the surface city of Duskhaven and the deeper Underdark. This makes them the primary intermediaries between the surface and underground societies.
* **Weaponized Neutrality:** The Ebon Web strictly maintains neutrality within the Duskhaven criminal underworld. They are willing to work with anyone. They use this neutral stance to play other criminal groups off of each other, ensuring that no single rival faction ever becomes dominant.
* **Primary Enterprises:** Their main sources of income are smuggling and trafficking operations.

## Secrets & DM Notes

* **The Cargo:** The exact nature of what the Ebon Web is trafficking from the Underdark into the surface world is highly classified. (DM discretion: it may involve dangerous illicit magic or creatures).
* **The Neutrality Lie:** Their "neutrality" is a front. They are actively manipulating the surface gangs to weaken them for a potential Underdark expansion.
