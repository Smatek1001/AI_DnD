# Nightshade Guild

**Summary:** The Nightshade Guild is a sophisticated and secretive criminal syndicate operating out of the labyrinthine streets of [[The Veil]].

## Operations & Headquarters

* **Modus Operandi:** Subterfuge, magical coercion, and blackmail. They deal in secrets and high-end illicit arcana.
* **Home Base:** They operate out of *[[The Whispering Door]]*, a tavern located in The Veil.
* **External Connections:** They have ties to independent operators in [[Market Square]], such as the halfling fence [[Mira "Quickfingers"]].

## Leadership Council

The guild is led by a formal council:

* **The Wraith (Leader):** A mysterious Drow who operates entirely from the shadows, exclusively using intermediaries to issue orders.
* **Thorne:** A human male who serves as the head of espionage.
* **Mara:** A female tiefling who specializes in arcana and magic.
* **Vrenna:** A female Drow council member.

## Ground Assets

* **Rynna:** A female goblin who works as a fence for the guild.
* **Kyssra & Jib:** Lookouts for the guild. Kyssra is a female Drow, and Jib is a male halfling.

## Secrets & DM Notes

* **The Wraith:** The Wraith's true identity is unknown even to the rest of the Leadership Council.
