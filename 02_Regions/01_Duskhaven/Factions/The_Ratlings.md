# The Ratlings

**Summary:** The Ratlings are a gang of street urchins operating in Duskhaven. To the city, they are just homeless children, but to Vaelin, they are a highly effective intelligence network, functioning as his eyes and ears on the streets.

## Operations & Headquarters

* **The Nest:** Their headquarters is located in the [[Shadow_Quarter]]. It is situated on the upper floor of the Cogsworth Tenement, which is an abandoned apartment building.
* **Street Intelligence:** The urchins function as lookouts across the city. They are a primary source for passing on rumors, making them an excellent vector for quest hooks.
* **Rivals:** The Ratlings are sometimes bullied by [[The Rust Dogs]], a petty criminal gang that operates out of the Docks.

## Key NPC: Finch (Leader)

* **Appearance & Persona:** Finch is a 14-year-old human girl. She is highly street smart and fiercely protective of her fellow Ratlings.
* **The Bond:** Vaelin rescued Finch from an abusive city guard. Because of this, she is fiercely loyal to Vaelin and sees him as a big brother. In turn, Vaelin is highly protective of her, viewing her as a little sister.

## ⚠️ DM Directives (Vaelin's Mechanics & Hooks)

* **The Hideout Exception:** Finch is the only person who has unrestricted access to [[The Black Oven]] (Vaelin's personal hideout). Mechanically, the DM must rule that she automatically bypasses all of his locks and traps, whether they are mundane or magical.
* **The "Payment":** Whenever Finch sneaks into his hideout, she steals his sweets and leaves behind odd trinkets as "payment". The DM should use these trinkets to add flavor, or occasionally leave a trinket that serves as a clue for a new mission.
