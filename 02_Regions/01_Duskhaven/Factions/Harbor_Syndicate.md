# Harbor Syndicate

**Summary:** The Harbor Syndicate is a heavy-handed criminal organization that operates strictly out of [[The Docks]].

## Operations & Locations

* **Modus Operandi:** Blunt force trauma, extortion, and intimidation. They are the loudest and most physically aggressive syndicate.
* **Home Base:** Their main headquarters is the Harbor Syndicate's warehouse.
* **The Front:** To conduct business with non-members and outsiders, they utilize a tavern in the Docks called the *[[Crow's Nest]]* as their primary meeting location.
* **External Assets:** They occasionally hire independent smugglers, like Rorick "Wavebreaker," to pick up small jobs.

## Leadership & Key Members

The top three leaders are pseudonymous and highly protective of their real identities:

* **The Anchor:** The leader. He is a male human with a muscular, imposing physical presence and a deep, resonant voice.
* **The Forger:** A male human document forger. He is skinny, pale, and has ink-stained fingers. He is nervous, paranoid, easily startled, twitchy, fidgety, obsessed with details, and a creature of habit. He maintains a collegial relationship with the independent gnome forger [[Silas "Inkfinger"]].
* **The Shade:** The spymaster. He is a male halfling who is both cunning and ruthless.

## Syndicate Muscle & Ground Assets

* **Korgal "Iron Fist":** The chief enforcer. He is a brutal Orc who is significantly smarter than he lets on.
* **Skarlix & Brak:** Skarlix is a male goblin loan shark, supported by his loan collector, a male half-orc named Brak.
* **Skrix:** A male goblin courier.

## Secrets & DM Notes

* **The Anchor's Identity:** The Anchor's true, legitimate identity within Duskhaven society is a heavily guarded secret that could shatter the city's political landscape if revealed.
