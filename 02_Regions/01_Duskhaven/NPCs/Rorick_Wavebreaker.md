# Rorick Wavebreaker

**Summary:** An independent smuggler who occasionally takes small jobs from the [[Harbor Syndicate]].

## Skills & Relationship to Vaelin

* **The Smuggler:** He knows the locations of various secret coves and caves in the surrounding area. He is capable of acquiring goods through smuggling networks and sneaking Vaelin in and out of [[Duskhaven]] entirely under the noses of city authorities.
* **Disposition:** He is friendly to Vaelin.

## Secrets & DM Notes

* **Double Dealing:** Rorick is deeply in debt to a rival faction (DM's choice) and occasionally skims cargo or sells shipping schedules to keep himself afloat. This makes him a potential liability or a plot hook.
