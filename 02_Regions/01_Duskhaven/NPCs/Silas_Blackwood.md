# Silas Blackwood

## Overview

Silas Blackwood is a high-end jeweler operating out of Market Square in Duskhaven. Outwardly, he is a polite, sharply dressed, and highly professional merchant catering to wealthy clients. In reality, he moonlights as a high-level fence and asset holder for the Nightshade Guild.

## Appearance & Personality

* **Appearance:** Impeccably dressed in tailored coats. He carries himself with a practiced, neutral grace and usually works with a jeweler's loupe in hand.
* **Personality:** Paranoid but greedy. He carefully weighs the risks of the illicit underworld against the immediate payout of wealthy buyers. He maintains a polite, guarded merchant's smile, rarely showing his true hand.

## Notable Details

* Silas has access to highly illegal and heavily warded Duergar technology.
* He sleeps in a spartan third-floor apartment above his shop and is a notoriously light sleeper.
* **Status:** Currently compromised. He was [[Session_02_Blackwood_Retrieval|robbed]] of a critical Society asset (the weeping willow signet ring) and a portion of his personal wealth by Vaelin Shadowleaf.
