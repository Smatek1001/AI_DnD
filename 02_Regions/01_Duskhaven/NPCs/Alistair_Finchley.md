# Alistair Finchley

**Summary:** Known as the "Archivist of Lost Words," Alistair is an elderly male human who operates as a highly skilled linguist and historian.

## Persona & Skills

* **Personality:** He is discreet and highly values punctuality. He does not suffer fools and is notoriously impatient with anyone who wastes his time.
* **Expertise:** He is a colleague of [[Elaris]] (from [[The Shaded Lantern]]) and is highly skilled in magic item identification.
* **DM Directive:** Given his linguistic and historical expertise, Alistair is the perfect NPC for the AI to use when Vaelin needs to translate ancient texts or uncover deep lore regarding [[Therandril]] or [[The Nameless One]].

## Secrets & DM Notes

* **Dangerous Knowledge:** Alistair knows far more about the Far Realm and the cosmic entities that threaten the Material Plane than he lets on. He carefully guards this knowledge to prevent drawing the attention of [[The Cult]].
