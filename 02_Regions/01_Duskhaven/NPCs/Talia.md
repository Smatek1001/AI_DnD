# Talia

## Overview

Talia is a high-ranking operative and handler for **[[The_Society|The Society]]**, operating primarily out of [[The_Shadow_Quarter|The Shadow Quarter]] in [[Duskhaven]]. She acts as the direct intermediary between field agents like Vaelin and the organization's ruling body, the Shadowy Council. She frequents a specific booth at *[[The_Drunken_Raven|The Drunken Raven]]*, using the tavern's ambient noise and unsavory clientele as cover for dispatching contracts and receiving stolen assets.

## Appearance & Personality

* **Appearance:** Unassuming but sharp. She favors dark, practical coats that easily conceal weaponry. Her eyes are highly observant, taking in minute details (like the smell of soot or the absence of distant alarms) in seconds.
* **Personality:** Cold, professional, and entirely devoid of unnecessary emotion or inflection. She does not flinch or react to sudden movements, and she keeps a tight rein on her operatives. While she is strict and demands absolute perfection (zero-footprint jobs), she shows genuine respect for clean, efficient work.

## Notable Details

* **Tradecraft:** She relies heavily on coded ledgers to track faction business and safehouses.
* **Preparedness:** Even when casually nursing a dark coffee, she is always braced for betrayal or combat, habitually resting her hand beneath the table near a concealed dagger when approached.
* **Resources:** She handles the operational payouts for The Society's contracts, carrying tightly drawn leather pouches of platinum and gold.
