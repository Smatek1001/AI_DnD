# The Black Oven (Vaelin's Hideout)

> [!SYSTEM DIRECTIVE]
> **For the AI Dungeon Master:** This is Vaelin's primary, off-the-books safehouse. It is completely unknown to The Society and The Cult of the Nameless One. The physical security and concealment are extremely high. If Vaelin is resting here, random encounters should be strictly limited to the streets below; the hideout itself is functionally secure unless Vaelin is explicitly tracked here by a high-level threat.

## Description

Located in the deeper, forgotten alleys of the Shadow Quarter, the hideout is situated in the reinforced attic space of a long-abandoned bakery. The ground floor is partially collapsed, choked with debris, and smells faintly of damp rot and century-old flour. To the casual observer, the structure looks entirely uninhabitable and structurally unsound, a deliberate facade maintained by Vaelin.

## Access & Security

Vaelin relies on his agility and stealth to bypass the mundane world to enter the hideout.

* **The Entrance:** There are no stairs leading to the attic. Access requires scaling the exterior brickwork in the alleyway and slipping through a false louver in the dormer window, or using the `Acrobatic Movement` / `Climber` features to navigate the ruined chimney shaft from the inside.
* **Mundane Traps:** The approach through the lower bakery is laced with caltrops beneath the dust and tripwires made of virtually invisible fishing line that trigger localized, muffled chimes in the attic.
* **The Dead Drop:** A loose brick in the alley wall below, marked with a microscopic chalk symbol of a rat, serves as a dead drop. Trusted allies (like Finch) can leave encrypted messages here without ever knowing how to get inside.

## The Attic Interior

The interior is a stark contrast to the ruined bakery below. It is cramped but meticulously clean, pragmatic, and organized.

* **The Roost:** A specialized, elevated rafter beam serves as a resting spot for Lirael, giving her a vantage point out the dormer window to watch the alleyway below.
* **The Armory:** A concealed floorboard stash contains Vaelin's spare mundane disguises, forgery kit supplies, and emergency rations.
* **The Escape Route:** A heavily greased trapdoor in the ceiling leads directly onto the slate roof. A coiled silk rope is pre-anchored here, allowing for an immediate, silent descent into an adjacent alley if the bakery is ever breached.

## Roleplay & Atmosphere

* **Lighting:** Vaelin relies entirely on his darkvision; there are no candles or lanterns used here, ensuring no light ever bleeds through the roof shingles.
* **Vibe:** Silent, cold, and calculated. It is a place of absolute paranoia and safety. The only sound is the distant, muffled rain of Duskhaven and the occasional shifting of the old timber.
