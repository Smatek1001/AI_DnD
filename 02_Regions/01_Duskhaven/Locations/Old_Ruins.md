# Old Ruins

**Summary:** A long-abandoned ancient Elf city located near Duskhaven. It is completely distinct from Therandril.

## Atmosphere & Lore

* **The Curse:** It is considered cursed and haunted by the locals, completely abandoned by polite society and heavily avoided by scavengers.
