# The Rusty Anchor

**Summary:** The Rusty Anchor is a tavern located in [[The Docks]] district of [[Duskhaven]]. It is a reliable, familiar haven for Vaelin, run by the closest thing he has to a surrogate family.

## Atmosphere & Rules

* **Smell:** Hearty fish stew, sea salt, spilled rum, and pipe weed.
* **Sound:** Boisterous sea shanties, roaring laughter, and the clattering of wooden mugs.
* **Vibe:** Warm, welcoming, but fiercely protected.
* **Neutral Ground:** The tavern is considered a sanctuary. The owner enforces strict neutrality and peace among rival gangs, syndicates, and patrons. Violence inside the tavern is swiftly and brutally dealt with.

## Notable NPCs (The Stonehearts)

### [[Greta "Mama" Stoneheart]]

* **Role:** Owner and operator of The Rusty Anchor.
* **Appearance & Persona:** She is an older, middle-aged female human who is gruff, no-nonsense, and highly respected in the Docks.
* **Relationship to Vaelin:** She is deeply motherly toward Vaelin. She took him in when he was a homeless orphan on the streets of Duskhaven.

### [[Elara "Lark" Stoneheart]]

* **Role:** Waitress at the tavern and Greta's daughter.
* **Appearance & Persona:** Lark is in her late teens, shy, kind, cute, and graceful. She hums while she works. She is a quarter-elf (human mother, half-elf father) and passes mostly as human, though she retains a slim, graceful build and slightly pointed ears.
* **Relationship to Vaelin:** She harbors a secret crush on Vaelin.

## ⚠️ DM Directives (Vaelin's Mechanics & Hooks)

* **The "Go Bag":** Vaelin maintains a hidden "go bag" in the back storeroom of the tavern for emergencies. Greta and Lark both know about it and secretly keep it stocked with fresh food for him. The DM should treat this as a secure cache that Vaelin can always access.
* **The Informant:** Because Lark is a quiet, graceful waitress serving a rough crowd, she frequently overhears valuable gossip and rumors. The DM should use Lark as a primary vehicle for delivering street-level quest hooks, secrets, and syndicate movements to Vaelin.

## Secrets & DM Notes

* **Greta's Suspicion:** Greta knows Vaelin is involved in dangerous, illicit work, though she does not know about [[The Society]]. She will violently protect his secret identity if questioned by the City Watch or rival gangs.
