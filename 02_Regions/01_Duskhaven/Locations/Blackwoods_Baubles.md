# Blackwood's Baubles

## Overview

Blackwood's Baubles is a high-end jewelry store located in the claustrophobic, narrow streets of Market Square. It is flanked by a weaver's shop, separated by a tight, three-foot alleyway.

## Layout & Security

The building is a three-story timber-and-brick structure with distinct security measures on each level:

### Ground Floor (Storefront)

* Features a glass display counter and velvet trays showcasing rings, necklaces, and timepieces.
* **Security:** The front door is warded with a dormant magical trap that activates when the shop is closed. A heavy fabric curtain separates the storefront from the back staircase.

### Second Floor (Workspace)

* A cluttered jeweler's workshop smelling of polishing oil, brass filings, and soot. Features a dormant jeweler's forge connected to an 8-inch iron exhaust pipe leading to the roof.
* **The Safe:** The corner houses a massive, Duergar-forged iron safe protected by a heavy Abjuration aura. It requires a specific, guttural Duergar command word to drop the ward, followed by a mechanical combination (3 left, 1 right, 2 left).
* **Security:** The windows overlooking the alley are rigged with thin copper wire. If the sash is forced up, the wire snaps and drops a heavy iron weight onto a glass bell.

### Third Floor (Apartment)

* Silas Blackwood's private, spartan living quarters.
* **Security:** Accessed via a narrow spiral staircase from the workspace. It features a small, hinged dormer window leading to the slanted slate roof, secured from the inside by a simple, non-magical iron lever.
