# The Veil

**Summary:** A labyrinthine district with narrow, twisting streets and a distinct noir aesthetic.

## Atmosphere

* **Smell:** Heavy incense, pipe smoke, and rain slicked on ancient cobblestones.
* **Sound:** Hushed whispers, the strike of matches, and distant, melancholic jazz-like instrumental music.
* **Vibe:** Mysterious, highly dangerous for outsiders, and perfect for clandestine meetings.

## Notable Establishments & NPCs

* **[[The Shaded Lantern]]:** A magic shop owned by [[Elaris]], an ancient and wise female high elf sage with a detached curiosity. She attended the college of [[Alistair Finchley]]. She is assisted by [[Lyra]], a female human.
* **[[The Crimson Coin]]:** An elite gambling den operated by [[Sterling Van]], a well-dressed and charismatic male human.

## Secrets & DM Notes

* **The House Always Wins:** The specific magical methods Sterling Van uses to rig games, launder money, or collect debts from high-profile losers are secret.
* **Elaris's Past:** Elaris's true connection to Alistair Finchley and what she knows of the deeper arcane workings of Duskhaven are closely held.
