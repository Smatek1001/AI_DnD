# Shadow Quarter

**Summary:** The poor and neglected area of the city, largely abandoned by the city watch and legitimate authority.

## Atmosphere

* **Smell:** Damp rot, cheap ale, and open sewage.
* **Sound:** Muffled arguments through thin walls, coughing, and the scuff of boots in muddy alleys.
* **Vibe:** Desperate, claustrophobic, and highly territorial.

## Notable Establishments & Turfs

* **[[The Drunken Raven]]:** A tavern where Vaelin receives his mission assignments from his handler, [[Talia]].
* **[[The Nest (Cogsworth Tenement)]]:** An abandoned apartment building known as the Cogsworth Tenement. The upper floor serves as the headquarters for [[The Ratlings]].
* **[[The Black Oven]]:** An abandoned bakery that secretly houses Vaelin's off-the-books attic safehouse.

## Secrets & DM Notes

* **Unseen Threats:** The Shadow Quarter occasionally serves as a hunting ground for things that creep up from the Sewers, events the city council aggressively ignores.
