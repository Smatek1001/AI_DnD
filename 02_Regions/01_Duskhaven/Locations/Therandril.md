# Therandril

**Summary:** Therandril was an ancient Elf city that straddled the border between the Material Plane and the Feywild. It was a sanctuary for Primal Elves and fell under the protection of the Archfey [[Caladwen]].

## Atmosphere (The Ruins)

* **Visual:** Shattered crystalline spires wrapped in writhing, unnatural, purple-veined vines. The sky above the ruins is permanently bruised with a sickly, aberrant aurora.
* **Sound:** Total, unnatural silence, occasionally broken by a sound that resembles a massive, wet heartbeat coming from deep underground.
* **Vibe:** A place of profound cosmic tragedy and wrongness. The Fey magic is actively fighting a losing battle against Far Realm corruption.

## History & Destruction

* **The Fall:** When Vaelin was young, the city was attacked and utterly obliterated by an ancient, Cthulhu-esque Elder God from the Far Realm known as [[The Nameless One]].
* **The Last Survivor:** Vaelin is the only known survivor of the attack, having been magically transported to the gritty, cosmopolitan coastal city of [[Duskhaven]] by Caladwen before he could be killed.
* **The Aberrant Taint:** The sheer cosmic horror of The Nameless One's attack permanently scarred the fabric of Therandril's magic, which is the source of the Aberrant corruption mixed into Vaelin's innate Warlock/Sorcerer spellcasting.

## Secrets & DM Notes

* **The Tether:** The Nameless One did not just destroy Therandril; it is currently feeding on the wound in the Weave left behind.
* **Caladwen's Status:** [[Caladwen]] is not dead, but her power is severely depleted from the attack and the effort of hiding Vaelin. She uses [[Lirael]] to monitor him because she cannot risk reaching out directly until he is strong enough (Level 3).
