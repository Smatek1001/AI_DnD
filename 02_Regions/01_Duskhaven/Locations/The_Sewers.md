# The Sewers

**Summary:** An extensive, hazardous sewer network beneath the city that connects directly to the ocean and the deeper Underdark. It functions as a secondary city for the underclass.

## Atmosphere

* **Smell:** Putrid waste, stagnant water, and the metallic tang of blood.
* **Sound:** Echoing drips, rushing water, and unsettling, unseen skittering in the dark.
* **Vibe:** Pitch-black, intensely hostile, and claustrophobic. Only the desperate or the deadly walk here.

## Notable Establishments & Turfs

* **[[Ebon Web Syndicate]] Domain:** The sewers are the primary operating ground for the Ebon Web Syndicate.

## Secrets & DM Notes

* **The Deep Connections:** The exact points where the sewers transition into the true, untamed Underdark, and what monstrous entities use those paths to enter Duskhaven, are closely guarded secrets.
* **Ebon Web Vaults:** The locations of the Syndicate's stashes and hideouts within the maze are hidden behind traps and illusions.
