# The Drunken Raven

**Summary:** A tavern located in the [[Shadow_Quarter]]. This is the primary meeting spot where Vaelin receives his mission assignments from his handler, [[Talia]].

## Atmosphere

* **Smell:** Sour ale, wet wool, and old sawdust.
* **Sound:** Low, gruff murmurs, the scraping of heavy wooden chairs, and the crackle of a poorly maintained hearth.
* **Vibe:** Suspicious and guarded. Patrons mind their own business, keep their heads down, and sit with their backs to the wall.

## Notable NPCs

* **[[Tarren]]:** The tavern bouncer. A male human who is stoic and laconic, but friendly to Vaelin.
* **[[Talia]]:** Vaelin's handler for [[The Society]]. (Usually found at a secluded booth in the back).

## Secrets & DM Notes

* **The Back Room:** The Raven serves as a front for deeper Society operations. The cellar contains hidden exits that lead directly into the upper levels of [[The Sewers]].
* **Tarren's Loyalty:** Tarren is not just a bouncer; he is a low-level Society asset tasked with keeping the meeting space secure and logging who comes and goes.
