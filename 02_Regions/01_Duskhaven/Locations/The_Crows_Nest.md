# The Crow's Nest

**Summary:** The Crow's Nest is a wide, two-story structure built from heavy, salt-stained maritime timber designed to withstand coastal storms. It operates as a tavern, but functions primarily as a meeting location and fortified front for the [[Harbor_Syndicate]].

## Atmosphere & Layout

* **The Exterior:** The front entrance features a heavy awning, usually guarded by at least two Syndicate enforcers. A blind side alley, often stacked with heavy ale kegs, provides a shadowed, unguarded perimeter.
* **The Ground Floor:** A raucous, smoky tavern area heated by a massive, roaring brick hearth. It is typically filled with a dozen or more Harbor Syndicate thugs and enforcers.
* **The Second-Floor Landing:** A staircase leads up from the tavern to a second-floor landing, which is guarded around the clock.

## The Shade's Office

Located on the second floor, this spartan and ruthlessly organized room serves as the private sanctum for [[The_Shade|The Shade]], the Syndicate's paranoid spymaster. It is sweltering due to the brick chimney venting from the hearth below.

### Security Measures & Tradecraft

The Shade is exceptionally paranoid, layering physical and magical security:

* **The Windows:** The front-facing window is heavily boarded over with iron-reinforced planks from the inside. The alley-facing window is secured by a pivoting iron latch rigged with a hair-thin silver tripwire.
* **The Abjuration Ward:** The alley window is further protected by a dormant, highly volatile Abjuration ward. The magic is anchored by a tiny sigil painted in powdered silver directly onto the inside of the latch.
* **The Decoy:** A thick, black leather book sits exposed in the center of the heavy oak desk, radiating a deceptive aura of Illusion magic to bait hurried thieves.

## Secrets & DM Notes

* **The Hidden Safe:** A solid steel lockbox is built flush into the heavy timber wall. It is concealed behind a tall iron filing cabinet that rests on deeply recessed, heavily greased casters running along polished grooves in the floorboards.
* **The Safe's Trap:** The safe features a heavy brass dial encased in a lethal cage of Abjuration magic, wrapped around a highly volatile Evocation core. Touching the dial without turning it to the correct combination will trigger a catastrophic explosion.
* **The Combination:** The combination to the safe is **14 Right, 32 Left, 7 Right**. The Shade keeps this written on a torn scrap of a nautical chart hidden inside a false-bottomed drawer in his desk. The brass dial also shows distinct micro-abrasions and wiped patina around these three numbers.
