# Noble Quarter

**Summary:** Located in the northwest, this district is the home of old money and the high-born of [[Duskhaven]].

## Atmosphere

* **Smell:** Perfumed gardens, expensive wine, and the faint ozone of magical security wards.
* **Sound:** Polite carriage wheels on clean cobblestones and distant, elegant string music.
* **Vibe:** Sterile, wealthy, heavily surveilled, and condescending.

## Notable NPCs & Locations

* **[[Lord Varyn]]:** A corrupt noble who is suave and charming, living in one of the sprawling estates.
* **[[Silas "Inkfinger"]]:** A middle-aged male gnome who operates a bookstore here. Vaelin is professionally aware that Silas operates as an underworld forger and alchemist. Silas maintains a collegial relationship with [[The Forger]] from the [[Harbor Syndicate]].

## Secrets & DM Notes

* **Silas's Ledgers:** While Vaelin knows Silas's profession, Silas's specific client list, financial ledgers, and private operations are a closely guarded secret.
* **Lord Varyn's Ties:** The specific criminal organizations and dark entities Lord Varyn aligns with to maintain his wealth are hidden behind layers of bureaucracy.
