# The Docks

**Summary:** The industrial shipping area on the south coast of [[Duskhaven]], full of warehouses, docks, and caravans.

## Atmosphere

* **Smell:** Brine, dead fish, tar, and heavy industrial sweat.
* **Sound:** Creaking ship hulls, shouting stevedores, and the clanging of ship bells.
* **Vibe:** Gritty, tense, and strictly business. It is a place of hard labor and brutal syndicate enforcement.

## Notable Establishments & Turfs

* **[[The Rusty Anchor]]:** A neutral-ground tavern run by [[Greta Stoneheart]].
* **[[Crow's Nest]]:** A tavern serving as the primary meeting location for the [[Harbor Syndicate]] to conduct business with non-members and outsiders.
* **[[Harbor Syndicate's Warehouse]]:** The heavily guarded home base for the [[Harbor Syndicate]].

## Secrets & DM Notes

* **Smuggling Routes:** The specific false-bottomed ships and underground tunnels the Harbor Syndicate uses to move contraband in and out of the warehouses are hidden.
