# 🌑 Duskhaven Chronicles: The Vaelin Shadowleaf Campaign

Welcome to the official repository for the **Duskhaven Chronicles**, a solo Dungeons & Dragons campaign driven by an AI Dungeon Master.

### 📜 The Narrative
Set within the grimy, treacherous streets of **Duskhaven**, this campaign follows the story of **Vaelin Shadowleaf**, a spellcaster bound by arcane pacts and watched closely by a mysterious patron, Caladwen. Accompanied by Lirael—a shapeshifting familiar with hidden motives of her own—Vaelin navigates a dangerous underworld of rival syndicates, from the extortionists of the Rust Dogs to the dark alleys of the Shadow Quarter. Survival in Duskhaven requires sharp blades, sharper wits, and a careful management of magical resources.

### ⚙️ The Engine (Obsidian + AI)
This repository is not just a collection of campaign notes; it is a fully interactive, live-state game engine built entirely within **Obsidian**. 

* **The Dynamic HUD:** Utilizing plugins like Dataview and Meta Bind, the vault acts as a video-game-style interface that actively tracks HP, spell slots, inventory, and dynamic skill synergies in real-time.
* **MemPalace Integration:** The lore, mechanics, and chronological session summaries are strictly formatted to feed a local RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) vector database. 
* **The GemDM:** By segmenting system scripts away from narrative text, this vault allows the AI Dungeon Master to read, recall, and dynamically roleplay within the world with incredible contextual consistency.
